Computer Science & Engineering

Computer Science Engineering is one of the most lucrative branches among all engineering streams. This branch studies the design, analysis, implementation and application of computation and computer technology. Computer Science is a breed of science that constitutes numerous fields like Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Crime, Network Security, Forensic Science, Neuro Science, Robotics, Image Processing, Copy right protection, simulation and modeling, business, law & medical etc.

Scope of Computer Science Engineering

This is an excellent field to have opportunities in software companies, web application, mobile gaming, video games, mobile applications, embedded system, virtual reality, animation, wireless network, enterprise computing, database system, computer security etc.

Jobs at Core companies for Computer Science Engineers

Government organizations like DRDO, ISRO, ONGC, ECIL, BHEL, SAIL, IRCTC, DMRC, IOCL, CHIPS, NIC etc. and numerous MNCs like Microsoft, Google, Yahoo, Amazon, IBM, Facebook, Oracle, Samsung, LG, Cisco, Infosys, TCS, WIPRO, Tech Mahindra, Accenture etc. are offering jobs to computer engineers.

Computer Science Engineering at SSIPMT

  • Highly qualified & experienced faculties.
  • Maximum faculties are GATE rankers/qualified.
  • Faculties mentor students for exams like GATE, OCJP, MCP, OCA, CCNA etc.
  • State-of-the-art labs, best infrastructure, dedicated facilities for AKASH Tablet Project, Spoken Tutorial and E-yantra – all powered by IIT, Bombay.
  • Department has professional tie-ups with companies like TCS, ORACLE, Softgalaxy, Iternet Business Japan to provide excellent training platform for students.
  • Department is associated with Professional bodies like CSI, IEEE and ACM to organize and host National level Workshops, Seminars, Student Conventions & Technical Competitions.
  • Majority of our ex-students are employed with multinational companies like TCS, WIPRO, Tech Mahindra, Persistent, Syntel, Accenture etc.

The Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs):

By the completion of Computer Science and Engineering program the student will have the following Program Specific Outcomes.

PSO - 1

An Ability to apply mathematical foundations, programming skills, algorithmic principles, data structures and theoretical computer science to model real world computer-based systems.

PSO - 2

An ability to demonstrate vital knowledge of Database System, Software Engineering, Cyber Security, Computer Networking and Operating System in the construction of Software applications.

PSO – 3

An ability to employ state-of-art methodologies for project using Artificial Intelligence(AI), Internet of Things (IOT) , App development and testing/validation with focus on optimization and quality related aspects.


The Program Educational Objectives (PEOs):

PEO’s - 1

Graduates will have a strong foundation in Mathematical, Scientific and Computer Engineering fundamentals necessary to formulate, solve and analyze Technical problems.

PEO’s - 2

Graduates will be equipped with Technical knowledge, Designing, Analysis and Professional attitude to solve Complex problems in Multidisciplinary environment, the solutions of which lead to significant societal benefits.

PEO’s – 3

Graduates will develop overall personality and will be competent in Managerial skills and Effective communication skill to be successful Industry-Ready Professionals as well as grow as Life-long Learners and Entrepreneurs.

Program Outcomes (POs):

PO1: Engineering Knowledge Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and an engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.
PO2: Problem Analysis Identify, formulate, research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences.
PO3: Design & Development of Solutions Design solutions for complex engineering problems and design system components or processes that meet the specified needs with appropriate consideration for the public health and safety, and the cultural, societal, and environmental considerations.
PO4: Investigations of Complex Problems Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of the information to provide valid conclusions
PO5: Modern Tool Usage Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.
PO6: Engineer and Society Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the professional engineering practice.
PO7: Environment and Sustainability Understand the impact of the professional engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate the knowledge of, and need for sustainable development.
PO8: Ethics Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities and norms of the engineering practice.
PO9: Individual and Team Work Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings.
PO10: Communication Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineering community and with society at large, such as, being able to comprehend and write effective reports and design documentation, make effective presentations, and give and receive clear instructions.
PO11: Project Management and Finance Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the engineering and management principles and apply these to one’s own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multidisciplinary environments.
PO12: Life-long Learning Recognize the need for, and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change.
